About The Course

What You'll Learn

Gain practical knowledge and skills to navigate the complexities of international marketing

Global Market Strategies

Learn how to develop and implement marketing strategies that work across different countries, cultures, and economic environments. Understand market entry modes and expansion tactics.

Consumer Behavior

Explore how cultural, social, and psychological factors influence consumer decision-making across different markets. Learn to identify and respond to diverse customer needs.

International Branding

Master the art of building and managing brands that resonate with international audiences while maintaining brand consistency across diverse markets.

Digital Marketing

Understand modern digital marketing tactics including social media, content marketing, SEO, and online advertising strategies for global reach.

Market Analysis

Develop skills in analyzing market trends, competitive landscapes, and identifying opportunities for business growth in local and international markets.

Pricing & Competition

Learn pricing strategies for different markets, understand global competition dynamics, and develop competitive advantages for sustainable business success.

Meet Your Professor

Learn from an experienced educator and industry expert

Professor Dr. Syed Mizanur Rahman

Associate Dean, Faculty of Business & Entrepreneurship

Professor Dr. Syed Mizanur Rahman is a distinguished academic leader serving as the Associate Dean of the Faculty of Business & Entrepreneurship (FBE) and Director of External Affairs at Daffodil International University (DIU). With over 16 years of dedicated service at DIU, he has made significant contributions to student mentorship, business education, and university development.

His expertise spans across marketing principles, global business strategies, and entrepreneurship development. As a life member of the Bangladesh Economic Association (BEA), he brings both academic rigor and practical insights to his teaching methodology.
